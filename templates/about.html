About BuildCo

Building excellence since 1985

Our Story

Founded in 1985 by John BuildCo, our company has grown from a small local contractor to one of the region's most respected construction firms.

With over 35 years of experience in the construction industry, BuildCo has established a reputation for delivering high-quality projects across various sectors, including commercial, residential, healthcare, educational, and industrial.

Throughout our journey, we've maintained our founding principles of quality craftsmanship, client satisfaction, and innovative solutions. Every project we undertake reflects our commitment to excellence and attention to detail.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional construction services that exceed client expectations through innovation, quality craftsmanship, and sustainable building practices.

We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of safety, integrity, and professionalism in every project we undertake, while fostering long-term relationships with our clients, partners, and communities.

Our Vision

To be the leading construction company known for transforming architectural visions into exceptional built environments that enhance communities and exceed client expectations.

We aspire to set new standards in the construction industry through innovative practices, sustainable solutions, and a relentless pursuit of excellence in everything we do.
